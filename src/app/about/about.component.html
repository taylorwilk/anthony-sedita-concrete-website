About us

At Anthony Sedita Concrete, we believe that quality craftsmanship and hard work are the foundation of every great concrete project. Founded by Anthony Sedita, a skilled tradesman with 17 years of experience in the concrete industry, our company is dedicated to delivering durable, high-quality work that enhances the value of homes and businesses across Maine.

Anthony grew up in Baldwin, Maine, where he developed a deep appreciation for creating things with his own two hands. Now based in Portland, he brings his passion for craftsmanship to every project, ensuring that each driveway, patio, and walkway is built to last. A true perfectionist, he takes pride in every detail, treating each job as if it were his own home.

Beyond concrete, Anthony has a love for woodworking, surfing, snowboarding, skateboarding, playing music, and the great outdoors. He feels incredibly fortunate to spend his days working outside, creating functional and beautiful spaces for his clients. His dedication to his craft and strong work ethic have earned him a reputation for reliability, precision, and outstanding customer service.

At Anthony Sedita Concrete, we don’t just pour concrete, we create lasting surfaces that stand the test of time. Whether you're looking to enhance your home with a new driveway, patio, or sidewalk, you can count on us to get the job done right.

Let’s bring your vision to life — one project at a time.
